About FutureWings

These free modules offer an honest look at how flight training works, what day-to-day life as a student pilot is like, and how to make confident decisions about your future in aviation. Whether you’re just exploring the idea of flying or already committed to this career, FutureWings is here to help you take your first step toward the skies.

The Creator of FutureWings

My name is Alejandro Inchausti, and I’m a flight instructor passionate about helping the next generation of pilots find their path in aviation. I created FutureWings to provide clear, beginner-friendly guidance for anyone curious about flight training and what it truly takes to become a pilot.
